The correct approach

Correct approach technique will improve your vertical!

Recieve

Communicate Effectively

Clear communication with teammates leads to smoother gameplay and fewer errors.

Don't give up

Stay focused on the game, do not give up and do your best!

More Volleyball Tips

  • Be ready and active at all times.
  • Keep your arms and hands flexible when setting the ball,.
  • Keep communication constant with your teammates during the game.
  • Always stay positive, no matter the game’s score.
  • Most important thing is practice, consistent practice will help you a lot more than any single tip.
